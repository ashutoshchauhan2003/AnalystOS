# AnalystOS Launch Content

## Professional LinkedIn Launch Post

I am excited to share AnalystOS, a free-core job-ready analytics lab for learners who want to move beyond passive learning and build visible proof of work.

AnalystOS helps learners go through a practical analyst workflow:

Diagnostic -> Learning Paths -> Labs -> Submission -> Review -> Portfolio -> Jobs

The MVP includes role diagnostics, structured paths for Data Analyst, Business Analyst, and Data Scientist learners, realistic labs, a SQL workspace, submissions, review flow, portfolio publishing, job matching, and recruiter-facing discovery.

The goal is simple:

Learn -> Practice -> Publish -> Get Hired

AnalystOS is built especially with Indian graduates in mind, where many students learn tools but still struggle to show job-ready proof to recruiters.

## Founder Story Paragraph

I am Ashutosh Chauhan, a PGDM Business Analytics & Marketing student building AnalystOS because I noticed a real gap: many graduates learn SQL, Excel, Python, dashboards, and analytics concepts, but they often do not have a clear way to practise realistic work, get structured feedback, and present recruiter-ready proof. AnalystOS is my attempt to turn analyst learning into a visible workflow: diagnose the right path, practise real tasks, submit work, get reviewed, publish a portfolio, and connect that proof to job opportunities.

## Recruiter-Focused Version

Recruiters often see resumes that list tools, courses, and certifications, but it is harder to inspect how a candidate thinks through a real analyst problem.

AnalystOS is designed to make candidate proof easier to evaluate.

Learners complete structured diagnostics, labs, submissions, reviews, and portfolio artifacts. Recruiters can then inspect skills, reviewed projects, recommendations, and job-fit signals instead of relying only on keyword claims.

Core flow:
Diagnostic -> Labs -> Review -> Portfolio -> Jobs
